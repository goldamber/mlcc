How do we help ocean creatures?

Research

We study different kind of animals in order to understand their needs and conservation status. This knoledge helps us to provide better conditions for auqatic creatures. All our research results we share with schools and universities.

Learn more

Shelter

We provide conservation options for rescued animals. We usually keep them for a couple of month depending on their health condition. However there are some individuals who were injured badly, so they are not able to come back to the wild.
